About

I'm a fourth-year PhD candidate at the University of Kurdistan, focusing on representation learning with an emphasis on multi‑view representation, multi‑label learning, and adversarial training. I'm a member of the Algebraic Machine Learning Team, and I currently serve as a thesis advisor to master's students at UoK. I hold a Master's degree in Artificial Intelligence from the UoK, where I worked with Dr. Fardin Akhlaghian, Dr. Alireza Abdollahpouri, and Dr. Amjad Seyedi on projects involving matrix factorization and adversarial training applied to multi‑label classification, multi‑view clustering, and link prediction. My educational background also includes a Bachelor's degree in Software Engineering from Razi University.
